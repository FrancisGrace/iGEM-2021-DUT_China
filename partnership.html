Partnership

TJUSLS_China

How did we meet

In April this year, after conducting literature research and actual social research, exchanges, and discussions with teachers and professional institutions, it was decided that the main body of this year's project would be the explanation of PET plastics. After the exchange of members, it was discovered that Tianjin University had also chosen the degradation of PET plastic as the research direction. Tianjin University's biological professional level is among the best in the country. As an influential university, exchanges and cooperation with it can promote the progress of our two sides' projects at the same time. So, in the end, our two sides have carried out long-term cooperation and exchanges.

Why we decided to do this cooperation

In the discussion group with them, we found that the interoperability between our projects is to degrade PET plastic.

In addition, our two teams also have their advantages. Our school has excellent synthetic biology and related research teachers who can help them get in touch with teachers and provide them with plasmid optimization experiments. As a mature iGEM team, TJUSLS_China can guide us in some aspects and provide us with experience and necessary materials.

According to many online exchanges between DUT_China and TJUSLS_China, we have reached a consensus on establishing a partnership since April. And our partnership is not limited to the exchange of the project process; we also provide more support for each other and promote the success of each other's projects.

TJUSLS_China and DUT_China are members of Plasticase, a community dedicated to solving the problem of plastic pollution. At the virtual round table "Reshaping" the Future, both parties introduced their projects. The other side congratulated us on our success. At the same time, We also send our blessings to DUT_China and hope that they can get good experimental results.
